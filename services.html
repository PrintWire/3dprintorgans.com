Organ Bioprinting Services

Custom-engineered organ constructs for transplantation research, drug testing, and regenerative medicine

WHAT WE OFFER

Our Expertise

Cardiac Tissue Constructs

Vascularized heart tissue models with embedded microchannels replicating native myocardial architecture. Used for cardiotoxicity screening, cardiac drug development, and heart failure research. Cardiomyocyte-laden bioinks with >85% post-print viability.

Liver Tissue Models

Hepatic constructs incorporating primary hepatocytes and stellate cells in zonated architectures that replicate liver lobule microstructure. Ideal for DILI (drug-induced liver injury) studies, ADME profiling, and metabolic disease modeling.

Kidney Organoids & Renal Models

Proximal tubule and glomerular constructs for nephrotoxicity testing and renal disease modeling. Patient-derived iPSC-based kidney organoids available for personalized medicine applications. Compatible with standard transwell and microfluidic platforms.

Custom Organ Engineering & R&D

Collaborative research partnerships for organ constructs beyond our standard catalogue — including lung, pancreas, and intestinal models. We co-develop print protocols, bioink formulations, and maturation conditions specific to your tissue target and assay endpoints.
