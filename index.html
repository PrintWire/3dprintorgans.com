Organ Model Services

01

Surgical Planning Models

Pre-operative replicas of patient anatomy for complex surgical rehearsal. Cardiac, hepatic, renal, and neurological models in multi-material prints simulating tissue stiffness gradients. Reduces intraoperative decision time and surgical risk.

02

Medical Education Specimens

High-fidelity anatomical teaching models replacing cadaveric specimens. Full organ sets, pathological variants, and developmental series. Durable, reusable, and legally unrestricted for global distribution.

03

Device & Implant Testing

Anatomically accurate phantoms for pre-clinical device validation. Cardiovascular flow loops, respiratory training models, and patient-matched implant fit-check tools. Material matched to target tissue compliance.

MODEL MATERIALS

Realistic Organ Materials

Anatomical accuracy requires more than geometry — tissue stiffness, color, and compliance must match the surgical reality.

  • ● Shore 30A silicone — liver, kidney, soft tissue compliance
  • ● Shore 60A rubber — cartilage and meniscus stiffness
  • ● Rigid polyurethane — cortical bone and calcified structures
  • ● Translucent elastomer — vascular and airway model clarity
  • ● Multi-material PolyJet — simultaneous rigid-soft construction
  • ● DICOM segmentation and model preparation service included
